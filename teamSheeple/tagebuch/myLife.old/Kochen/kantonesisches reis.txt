

                                      Cantonese rice
                          Huo Tui Dan Chao Fan - Easy - 25 min. 

                                       Ingredients :

                         1 new onion;
                         50g smoked ham (two thick cut slices);
                         3-4 eggs;
                         salt; 
                         5-6 S oil;
                         300g boiled Basmati rice;

Cantonese rice

                     Instructions:

                       1.Mince the new onion. Chop the ham in dices. Whip
                         the eggs and heat them (omelet).
                       2.Heat oil in a wok add the omelet the onion and the
                         ham stirring well. 
                       3.Add the rice and stir. Serve when heat.
