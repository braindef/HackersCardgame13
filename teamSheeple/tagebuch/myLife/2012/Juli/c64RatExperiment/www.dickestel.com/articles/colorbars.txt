MONITORS ADJUSTING BARS

by John Lindeman

Monitors rarely need replacement but occasionally you may want to adjust
the color. Here is a color bar program to help you do it.

     10 FOR X=l to ll: READA
     20 FOR Y=1 to 80
     30 PRINT CHR$(A); "(RVS-ON) (SPACE)";:
     40 NEXT: NEXT: END
     50 DATA 5,28,30,31,144,156,158,159,150,149,153
