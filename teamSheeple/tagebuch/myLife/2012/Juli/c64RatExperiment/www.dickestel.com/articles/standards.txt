Standards in Conflict

The International Standards Organization and the International Electrotechnical Commission this year
(1998) designated Oct. 14 as World Standards Day. The United States celebrated World Standards
Day on Oct. 11; Finland celebrated on Oct. 13; and Italy celebrated on Oct. 18. The "networked
society" clearly needs to get its signals in sync. 

A report from Washington editor Jodie T. Allen in the on-line magazine Slate
